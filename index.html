About Us

We are an exporting company from Indonesia, which sells cinnamon sticks, powder, broken, essential oil, and cinnamon extract products. in trading we always prioritize the quality of our products and prioritize buyer satisfaction.

  • Our products are of good quality
  • Our product is number one in Indonesia
  • Our products are always ordered back by buyers

One thing that becomes the principle of our company is to be a trusted company in the world, we really put customer's trust in us, and we always do the best for our customers.
We do the best of the best.

Why us ? because we provide the best for our customers

How can we do the best for our customers ?

  • 01 Where do our products come from?

    Our products are from farmers in Indonesia, where their products have been sold in almost all countries such as America, Russia and England.

  • 02 How can we guarantee the quality of our products?

    we always sort our products before we sell them to buyers, we separate the good and bad quality products. Of course, what we sell is good quality.

  • 03 Has our product passed the lab test?

    Our products are always lab tested before we sell them to buyers, lab tests to ensure our products do not carry disease, pests, and bacteria and are safe for consumption.
